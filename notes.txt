Rules: Use mouse click to elevate the plane with the force of gravity.  Avoid thunder clouds that could harm your plane and take away a life. 
Collect gas cans to increase fuel in the plane allowing it to not lose all of it along the way.  Collect coins to increase score.


Goal: Get the highest score possible flying without running out of gas or hitting a thunder cloud and losing all 3 lives.


Story: You are flying in a plane and you see these gold coins and try to grab them but dangerous thunder clouds stand in your way to the gold coins, your fuel is also
decreasing while flying so you try to collect these fuel cans that are falling from the sky due to some odd phenomenon.
